Formal Biography

Ben Mesnik is currently a Machine Learning Engineer at State Farm. He previously founded a startup, where he built an AI coach for gaming. He transformed the concept into a platform which helped over 1 million gamers improve (Demo). Prior to founding Tip Genius, Ben worked as a software engineer at Constant Contact, where he helped build internet-scale systems and applications. Ben holds a degree from Lehigh University (BS '20, Computer Science), plus his formal education has been enriched by over a decade of entrepreneurial ventures and self learning. Ben has built dozens of software products from 0-1 and has worked across Machine Learning, AI, Gaming, Frontend, Backend, DevOps, Data, Mobile, and Fintech domains. Ben also spent several years traveling the world as a digital nomad, plays soccer religiously, and skis 30 days per year on average.
